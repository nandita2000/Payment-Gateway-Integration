ABOUT US

‘Voice of Stray Dogs’ aka Pawdonation Sanctuary and Hospital was founded on the principle that no dog will ever be euthanised because of lack of space, money or resources. Since we started, no dog at Pawdonation ever has been. Each dog has the best, most sophisticated and most expensive, medical care in India.

SUPPORT US

It is not easy to do what we do, but you can help us care for our dogs. Monthly expenses to run the Pawdonation Sanctuary are extremely high, and your support helps us provide the best care for our resident dogs and fund Pawdonation stray dog rescues and sterilisation projects across India.
